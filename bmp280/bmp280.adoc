== Specification

* Voltage: 1.71 - 3.6v
* Peak current: 1.12 Ma
* Temperature: -40C - +85C

== Wiring

Arduino    BMP280
GND   ---  GND
3.3v  ---  VCC
A4    ---  SDA
A5    ---  SCL
